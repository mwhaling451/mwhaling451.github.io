All About Java Been!


Java Been Coffee Co. was founded in 2015 in the kitchen of two coffee lovers! What started out as bringing coffee to coworkers turned into a full-time coffee business! All of our beans are responsibly harvested from Colombia, and are sold pre-ground or as whole beans at local stores!


Our Vision

We want to be able to provide flavorful coffee to home across the country, to give people the taste of their favorite cup of coffee each and every day.
